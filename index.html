The Story Behind My Journey

It all began with a casual gaming session with friends. Little did I know, that one game would spark a passion for competitive play. When the pandemic hit, I dedicated my time to gaming, taking it to the next level by competing in minor and major tournaments. Today, I am representing Gearhawks Esports, an esports team in TUP Manila, in various competitive MLBB tournaments.

I've been fascinated with online gaming since childhood, and it's been a dream of mine to excel in this field. I hope that my past self would be proud of what I've achieved so far.
